What Is A Valid Withdrawal?

The User Must Have installed And Used The App Continuously Atleast For A Period Of 5 Days After Requesting A Withdrawal.
User Must Not Have used Any Illegal Scripts/files To Increase His/Her Balance.
The User Must Have Given A "5 Star Rating With Good Review" Not Less Than 12 Words On Playstore.If You Haven't Done This Yet, You May Do It Now.



The User Must Have A Dollar Greater Than The Withdrawal Amount In His/Her Balance. For Example, An User Must Have 21$ In His Balance To Make A Withdrawal Request Of 20$ .
The Withdrawal Request That Fullfills The Above Requirements Is Called A Valid Withdrawal.
